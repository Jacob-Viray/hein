Collection 2017 - 2018

This body of work includes ceramic pieces and paintings on watercolour paper, painted with natural dye and made from hand picked plant life. Tools become important when being more cautious of where resources come from and how they are used. A handmade ceramic paintbrush with human hair of the artist is used as a tool for painting.
